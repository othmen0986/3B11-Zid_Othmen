About ACV Tool

This ACV tool is a comprehensive platform designed to assist professional engineers in assessing their skills, setting career goals, and planning their professional development.

Profile

The profile section allows you to input and manage your personal and professional information, including education, work experience, and certifications.

Skills Assessment

Assess and rate your engineering skills to identify areas of strength and areas that may need improvement. The tool will generate a personalized skills development plan based on your assessment.

Career Development

Explore job opportunities, receive personalized career guidance, and access resources to enhance your professional growth. The tool provides suggestions for courses, certifications, and training programs to align with your career goals.
